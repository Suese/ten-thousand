Custom Sound Overrides
======================

Drop audio files into this folder using the filenames below to override the
built-in synthesized SFX. If a file is absent, the procedural version still
plays — you only need to add the ones you want to replace.

Only .mp3 is looked up — one HTTP request per slot. Convert any .wav / .ogg
sources to .mp3 before dropping them in here. If a slot's file is missing,
the procedural synthesized version plays.

The whole thing is gated by the in-game mute button (top-right 🔊 / 🔇)
and respects the master volume just like the synthesized sounds.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Game sounds
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  xx click          Tactile feedback on every button click.
  xx pluck          Generic short pluck (small confirmations).
  xx dice_shake     Rattle of the dice "cup" while holding Roll/Keep (loops ~320 ms).
  xx toss_dice      Quick whoosh at the moment the dice are thrown.
  xx dice_mat       A die thumping the felt.
  dice_dice      A die clacking into another die.
  select         Selecting a die during awaiting_keep.
  deselect       Deselecting a die.
  score          Small score confirmation (any commit under 500 pts).
  score_big      Big score fanfare (500+ pts, three 1s, etc.).
  xx bust           Sad trombone — turn busted.
  bank           Cha-ching coin jingle for banking points.
  hot_dice       Triumphant chord — all 5 dice scored, rerolling all 5.
  xx win            End-of-game winner fanfare.
  xx turn_start     "Your turn" cue at the top of a turn.
  xx turn_skipped   "Womp womp" — player ran out of time and got auto-passed.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Shop item sounds
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  xx flick          Slap — Flick item activation.
  xx ice_rink       Sword unsheathing — Ice Rink item activation.
  weighted       Heavy thump — Weighted Die item activation.
  xx dookie         Obnoxious fart — Dookie Throw item activation.
  xx tornado        Table-saw-cutting-wood — Tornado item activation.
                 (Loops? No — plays once. Keep it ~3-4 s for the full tornado.)
  tornado_hit    Big "WHACK!" when the tornado die collides with another die.
  portable_hole  Ominous descending vortex — Portable Hole item activation.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Examples
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  sounds/dookie.mp3          ← your custom fart
  sounds/tornado_hit.wav     ← your custom WHACK
  sounds/bank.ogg            ← your custom cha-ching

That's it. Hard-refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R) after adding files so the new audio is
fetched and decoded.
